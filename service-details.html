How I can help

I bridge business operations and technology—optimizing sourcing, logistics, and data workflows while building practical tools your team can maintain.

Procurement & Supply Chain Consulting

Improve cost, quality, and delivery reliability with structured procurement and logistics practices tailored to your context (public or private sector).

  • Category strategies, supplier prequalification, and contract frameworks
  • Lead-time reduction, demand planning, and inventory optimization
  • Vendor performance scorecards and SLA monitoring

Information Systems Advisory

Align systems with business goals—from requirements to selection and rollout—grounded in my MSc Information Systems background.

  • Process mapping, gap analysis, and solution evaluation
  • Lightweight automation (forms, workflows, integrations)
  • Change management and user enablement

Data & Analytics Dashboards

Clear, decision-ready dashboards for procurement, warehousing, and support operations—built with maintainability in mind.

  • KPIs: lead time, stockouts, vendor OTIF, savings tracking
  • ETL basics: clean, join, and refresh operational data
  • Delivery in Excel/Google Sheets or BI tools (as available)

Computer Vision (CNN) Prototyping

Research-driven prototypes—like my PhD work using CNNs to assist Foot-and-Mouth Disease diagnosis in Uganda—for proof-of-concepts and pilot studies.

  • Dataset curation, augmentation, and ethics-aware handling
  • Transfer learning baselines, metrics, and explainability (e.g., Grad-CAM)
  • Edge/low-power deployment experiments

Training & Mentorship

Practical workshops for teams and students on procurement fundamentals, analytics, and introductory machine learning workflows.

  • Curriculum tailored to your tools and capacity
  • Hands-on exercises and reference templates
  • Follow-up support and office hours
